Important Things in Life

  1. Have a clear life purpose
  2. Do what matters, not just what you like
  3. Avoid doing things based on mood
  4. Limit gaming or scrolling to under 15 minutes
  5. Don’t let emotions affect your duties and responsibilities
  6. Financial management
  7. Invest after building sufficient income
  8. Don’t buy things more expensive than your income before saving
  9. Don’t think you are smarter than others
  10. Don’t be afraid to fail Failure is a lesson

How to Improve Your Life

  • Have a clear life goal
  • Use the SMART method (Specific, Measurable, Actionable, Realistic, Timely)
  • Stop making excuses
  • Be brave enough to take calculated risks
  • Prioritize movement and growth
  • Manage finances: track all income and expenses in detail; money is not the goal, but a tool

Train Your Brain

  • Procrastination is not an option
  • Starting is hard, so prepare everything in advance
  • Use the 2-minute rule
  • Count 1–5 before starting
  • Reward yourself after completing tasks

College Tips

  • Join internships
  • Join organizations
  • Learn additional skills
  • Do freelance work
  • Build connections

Fatal Mistakes

  • Lack of experience
  • Wasteful lifestyle
  • Debt or online loans
  • Investing too late
  • Bad social circle

How to Be Respected

  1. Never be afraid to make eye contact
  2. Walk with confidence
  3. Relax and be yourself
  4. Pay attention when others are speaking
  5. Smile
  6. Show genuine interest in others
  7. Avoid being fake
  8. Talk less about yourself
  9. Dress neatly and attractively
  10. Do something unexpected
  11. Speak slowly and kindly

  19. Write your desires daily
  20. Limit sugar intake

  22. Make your bed after waking up
  23. Clear negative thoughts, stay positive
